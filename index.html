Barcelona, Spain

My first experience ever teaching was in Barcelona, Spain in a local high school. This was where I realized that teaching was my passion and I want to dedicate my life to this profession.

Guadalajara, Mexico

I taught children in Xiamen, China for a year, and this is where I really learned how to be a Teacher. I spoke little Chinese, so being forced to teach English without using the students' first language give me the skills I needed to excell as an educator!
