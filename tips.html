Activity Box

Making an Activity Box is a great way to make sure you are always prepared. In the box, you can put different kinds of card games that will allow you reinforce any grammar point at any time. Activites boxes are also great when you have extra time, and when you are asked to sub a class without any notice!

For a list of free card games and activities to put into your box, check out my free materials page.

Placemat

Place mats are the perfect activity to faciliate a debate in an English classroom. The procedure is simple:

  1. Give students a topic to debate about. For example, "Should school unfiforms be mandatory?"
  2. Give each student a post-it and tell the class that they have to write their opinion about the topic in one or two sentences.
  3. Finally, put the students in groups of 4. Each student must read his or her opinion, and then put their post-it in a corner of the place mat. Once each student has finished sharing, the group must talk and try to reach some sort of consensus.
